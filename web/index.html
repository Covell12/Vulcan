The process

From photo to part in five steps

No CAD skills, no calipers-and-guesswork. Just show us the spot and confirm a few measurements.

  1. 📸

    Snap

    Photograph where the part needs to go — the bracket wall, the broken knob, the gap.

  2. ✎

    Trace

    Circle or trace the area right on the photo so we know exactly what you mean.

  3. 📏

    Measure

    Answer a couple of measurement questions. Fit-critical dims are always confirmed by you.

  4. 🧊

    Preview

    Spin the real 3D model, see it ghosted to scale in your own photo, approve the render.

  5. 🔥

    Forge

    We print it on demand and ship it. STEP / 3MF / STL are yours too.
